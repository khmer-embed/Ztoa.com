ZaAlerts

Za provides an easy way to create predefined alert messages:

Success!

Indicates a successful or positive action.

Info!

Indicates a neutral informative change or action.

Warning!

Indicates a warning that might need attention.

Danger!

Indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.

Alerts are created with the .alert class, followed by one of the four contextual classes .za-alert-success, .za-alert-info,
.za-alert-warning or .za-alert-danger:

<div class="alert za-alert-success">Info! Indicates a neutral informative change or action.</div>
<div class="alert za-alert-info"> Info! Indicates a neutral informative change or action.</div>
<div class="alert za-alert-warning">Warning! Indicates a warning that might need attention.</div>
<div class="alert za-alert-danger">Danger! Indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.</div>
